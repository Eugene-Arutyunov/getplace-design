Which chains are winning the food delivery race in Manchester?

At Getplace, we continuously monitor the top 15 cities across the UK, like Manchester, to help restaurant brands stay on top of market dynamics. Our detailed reports reveal shifts in demand, category trends, and performance benchmarks against competitors + delivery spending data heatmaps.

For example, in May, Wingstop UK (Lemon Pepper Holdings Ltd) led the pack in Manchester, outperforming even the largest global chains. And look at Burgerism? With only 4 stores in the city, they are getting close to giants like McDonald's.

These insights support our partners in making smarter expansion, marketing, and investment decisions — based on data, not guesswork.

When you have access to delivery spend data for every restaurant in London, it’s fascinating to observe how demand shifts over time. You start to see who’s growing, who’s losing momentum, and how specific markets — like Spitalfields — are turning into major order magnets. These hubs create a win-win dynamic for restaurants, couriers, customers, and the platform alike.
